Anchovies on Pizza

Hello everyone! This delicacy has been debated since it's inception. This recipe will surely sway you to enjoy this outside the box pizza rendition.

So, get out your tackle box and get ready to tackle this amazing dish!

Ingredients/ Tools

  • Boat
  • Anchovies
  • Fishing Net
  • Cheese
  • Dough
  • Olives
  • Yellow Wellies and Raincoat

Directions

  1. Put your yellow wellies and raincoat on and hop on your boat
  2. Cast your net and catch some anchovies
  3. Season your chovies with whatever seasoning you prefer and set aside
  4. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  5. Assemble pizza and place in the over for 15 minutes
  6. Your pizza is now ready to enjoy!
