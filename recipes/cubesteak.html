CubeSteak

Here's a recipe that is delicious and nutritious!

A simple meal that will keep you from being a simpleton.

Ingredients

  • Cubes
  • Colored squares
  • Hands
  • Brain
  • Patience

Directions

  1. Heat a large pan on high heat
  2. Dump some patience in the pan and bring to a simmer
  3. Consume the patience once it has become like a syrup
  4. Now that you have some patience, sear your Rubiks on all sides
  5. The Rubiks is now prepped and ready to solve!
